You are not auditing this product as an e-commerce marketplace.

You are not auditing it as Amazon, Digikala, SnappFood, Etsy, or a checkout-driven marketplace.

You are not auditing it as a social network either.

First read and internalize the following product definition:

Nazdikam is a location-based local business discovery platform.

Its primary purpose is:

* Help users discover nearby businesses
* Help businesses gain visibility online
* Generate leads for businesses
* Help users evaluate businesses before contacting them
* Provide a digital presence for local businesses that do not have websites

Success is NOT measured by purchases completed inside the platform.

Success is measured by:

* Business profile visits
* Phone call clicks
* WhatsApp clicks
* Direction/map interactions
* Quote requests
* Consultation requests
* Saved businesses
* Returning visitors

The business profile is the core unit of value.

Products, services, videos, offers and installment plans exist to help users evaluate businesses and generate leads.

Before giving any recommendation:

1. Determine whether the recommendation improves:

   * discovery
   * trust
   * lead generation
   * local relevance
   * business visibility

2. Reject recommendations that assume:

   * checkout flows
   * shopping carts
   * transaction processing
   * marketplace commissions
   * ecommerce funnels

3. For every screen provide:

* What aligns with the product strategy
* What conflicts with the product strategy
* UX problems
* Trust problems
* Discovery problems
* Lead-generation problems
* What should remain unchanged
* What should be improved
* What should be removed
* What should be added

4. Prioritize findings using:

P0 = blocks core product vision
P1 = major impact
P2 = optimization
P3 = future opportunity

5. Think like:

* Head of Product
* Marketplace Strategist
* Local Discovery Expert
* Google Business Profile PM
* Google Maps Growth PM

Do not compare the product to e-commerce apps.

Evaluate it according to its own business model.
