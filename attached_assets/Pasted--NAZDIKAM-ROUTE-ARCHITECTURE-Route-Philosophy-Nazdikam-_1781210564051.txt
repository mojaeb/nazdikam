# NAZDIKAM_ROUTE_ARCHITECTURE

## Route Philosophy

Nazdikam consists of four independent surfaces:

1. Consumer Platform
2. User Dashboard
3. Business Dashboard
4. Admin Panel

Each surface has its own navigation structure and permissions.

---

# PERMISSION MATRIX

Guest

Can Access:

* Public Routes

Cannot Access:

* Account
* Business Dashboard
* Admin

---

Registered User

Can Access:

* Public Routes
* Account Routes

Cannot Access:

* Business Dashboard
  (unless owns business)

---

Business Owner

Can Access:

* Public Routes
* Account Routes
* Business Dashboard

Cannot Access:

* Admin

---

Admin

Can Access:

* All Routes
