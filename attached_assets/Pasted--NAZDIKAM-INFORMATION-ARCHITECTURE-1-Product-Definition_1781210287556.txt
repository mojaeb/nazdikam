# NAZDIKAM_INFORMATION_ARCHITECTURE

## 1. Product Definition

Nazdikam is a Local Discovery & Business Visibility Platform focused on Northern Iran.

The platform helps:

* Users discover nearby businesses
* Businesses gain digital visibility
* Users evaluate businesses before contacting them
* Businesses generate leads
* Traditional local businesses establish an online presence

Nazdikam is NOT:

* An e-commerce marketplace
* A checkout platform
* A commission-based marketplace
* A transaction processing platform

Success is measured by:

* Business profile visits
* Phone call clicks
* WhatsApp clicks
* Direction/map interactions
* Quote requests
* Consultation requests
* Saved businesses
* Returning visitors
* Subscription purchases

---

# 2. Core Product Principles

## Principle 1

Business Profile is the Core Unit of Value.

Everything exists to improve the visibility and effectiveness of the Business Profile.

## Principle 2

Products and Services are Evidence.

Products and services are not the destination.

They help users evaluate a business.

## Principle 3

Lead Generation is Conversion.

Conversion includes:

* WhatsApp click
* Phone call
* Quote request
* Consultation request
* Direction request
* Profile save

## Principle 4

Discovery Before Transaction.

Users discover and evaluate businesses first.

Transactions may happen outside the platform.

# 3. User Roles

## Guest User

Can:

* Browse businesses
* Browse products
* Browse services
* Browse videos
* Use search
* Use map
* View business profiles

Cannot:

* Save items
* Follow businesses
* Leave reviews
* Register a business

---

## Registered User

Can:

* Save businesses
* Save products
* Save services
* Follow businesses
* Leave reviews
* View history
* Register a business

---

## Business Owner

A Registered User who has created at least one business.

Can:

* Access Business Dashboard
* Manage profile
* View analytics
* Manage content
* Manage subscription
* Invite referrals

---

## Subscriber

A Business Owner with an active paid subscription.

Can:

* Publish content
* Edit content
* Create videos
* Create offers
* Create installment plans
* Access advanced analytics
* Receive premium visibility

---

## Administrator

Can:

* Verify businesses
* Moderate content
* Manage subscriptions
* Manage referrals
* Manage users

---
