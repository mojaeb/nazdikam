# NAZDIKAM — ACCOUNT DASHBOARD & BUSINESS DASHBOARD UX REBUILD

## HIGH PRIORITY REFACTOR

The current dashboards do not match the final product vision.

The current implementation feels like a SaaS admin panel, CRM, or ERP system.

Nazdikam is not an ERP.

Nazdikam is a Mobile First social-commerce platform.

The dashboards must be redesigned according to the uploaded wireframes.

---

# CORE UX PRINCIPLES

## Principle 1

Mobile First.

All dashboard decisions must be optimized for mobile screens first.

Desktop is only an expanded version of the mobile experience.

Do not create a different visual language for desktop.

---

## Principle 2

Users must never feel lost.

Each dashboard must have a single clear purpose.

Avoid visual clutter.

Avoid excessive cards.

Avoid excessive statistics.

Avoid complex management panels.

---

## Principle 3

Action First.

The user should immediately understand:

* Where am I?
* What can I do?
* What is my next step?

within 3 seconds.

---

# PRODUCT & SERVICE UNIFICATION

Current implementation is inconsistent.

Products and Services must become one system.

---

## Single Entity Model

Create a shared Item architecture.

Type:

* Product
* Service

Only the label changes.

The structure remains identical.

---

## Shared Form

Use one form component.

Fields:

* Title
* Description
* Images
* Category
* Price

---

## Discount Section

Toggle:

Enable Discount

If enabled:

* Discount percentage
* Expiration date

---

## Installment Section

Toggle:

Enable Installment

If enabled:

* Number of installments
* Installment amount
* Total installment price
* Eligible groups
* Conditions

---

# REMOVE DUPLICATED BUSINESS DATA

Do not ask users to enter:

* Phone number
* Address
* WhatsApp
* Website
* Instagram

inside Product or Service forms.

These values must inherit from the Business Profile.

---

# BUSINESS DATA INHERITANCE

Every Product/Service automatically uses:

* Business phone
* Business WhatsApp
* Business address
* Business location
* Business social links

The user enters these once only.

---

Replace ugly dropdowns with:

Bottom Sheet Selectors

Behavior:

* Slide from bottom
* Large touch targets

* Searchable

* Province selection

---

# FINAL SUCCESS CRITERIA

A new user should be able to:

1. Login
2. View account dashboard
3. Register a business
4. Enter business dashboard
5. Create a product/service
6. Enable discount
7. Enable installment
8. Publish

without confusion and without training.

If the experience feels similar to Instagram and modern mobile apps, the redesign is successful.
