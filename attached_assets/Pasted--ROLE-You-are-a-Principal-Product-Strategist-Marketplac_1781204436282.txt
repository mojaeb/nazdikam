# PRODUCT VISION

The platform is called "Nazdikam".

Nazdikam is a Location-Based Discovery Platform for Local Businesses.

Its purpose is to connect users with nearby businesses, services, products, offers and installment opportunities.

The platform initially focuses on Northern Iran provinces and later expands nationally.

---

# BUSINESS MODEL

Nazdikam is NOT primarily a checkout platform.

Nazdikam is primarily:

1. Discovery Platform
2. Lead Generation Platform
3. Local Business Visibility Platform
4. Business Profile Platform
5. Product & Service Showcase Platform
6. Installment Discovery Platform
7. Local Content Platform

The platform creates value by helping users discover and connect with local businesses.
