# BUSINESS PROFILE PAGE (NAZDIKAM OFFICIAL STRUCTURE)

## Core Principle

The Business Profile Page is the most important page in the platform.

Its purpose is:

1. Build Trust
2. Showcase Business
3. Generate Leads
4. Convert Visitors Into Contacts

The page must be mobile-first.

---

# HEADER SECTION

## 1. Cover Banner

A large fixed cover banner similar to:

* Twitter / X
* LinkedIn Company Pages

Purpose:

* Visual identity
* Branding
* Professional appearance

---

## 2. Business Logo

Circular profile image.

Requirements:

* Positioned partially over the banner
* Similar to Twitter profile layout
* Approximately 50% over banner
* Approximately 50% over content area

---

## 3. Follow Button

Position:

Top-right beside the logo area.

States:

* Follow
* Following

Must remain visible without scrolling.

---

# BUSINESS IDENTITY SECTION

## 4. Business Name

Display:

* Business Name
* Verification Badge (only if verified)

Verification badge must never be displayed unless verification workflow is completed.

---

## 5. Business Category

Display directly below business name.

Examples:

* رستوران
* فروشگاه پوشاک
* آموزشگاه
* کلینیک زیبایی

Only primary category is required.

---

# SOCIAL PROOF SECTION

## 6. Statistics Bar

Display:

* Followers Count
* Reviews Count

Example:

1,240 دنبال‌کننده
186 نظر

No other metrics needed here.

---

# STICKY NAVIGATION

## 7. Sticky Tab Menu

After scrolling:

The tab navigation must remain fixed at the top.

Purpose:

Quick navigation through the profile.

Tabs:

* ویدیوها
* محصولات / خدمات
* اطلاعات تماس
* آدرس و مسیریابی
* ساعت کاری
* راه‌های ارتباطی
* امتیاز و دیدگاه‌ها

Behavior:

Clicking a tab:

* Scrolls smoothly to the section
* Highlights active tab
* Keeps tab bar sticky

---

# CONTENT SECTIONS

## 8. Videos Section

Purpose:

Highest-trust content.

Display:

* Horizontal scroll
* Video thumbnails
* Fullscreen player on click

Priority:

High

---

## 9. Products / Services Section

Products and Services must be displayed in one unified section.

Requirements:

Filter:

* همه
* محصولات
* خدمات

Cards use the unified Product/Service Card design.

Clicking a card opens:

Product Detail Page
or
Service Detail Page

Both use the same detail layout.

---

## 10. Contact Information Section

Display:

* Phone Number
* Secondary Phone (optional)

Quick actions:

* Call
* Copy Number

---

## 11. Address & Navigation Section

Display:

* Province
* City
* Full Address

Actions:

* Open Map
* Get Directions

Priority:

High

This section directly affects lead generation.

---

## 12. Working Hours Section

Display:

Weekly schedule.

Examples:

شنبه
08:00 - 18:00

جمعه
تعطیل

If business is currently open:

Show green status.

If closed:

Show red status.

---

## 13. Communication Channels Section

Display only available channels:

* WhatsApp
* Telegram
* Instagram
* Website

Each item should be one-tap accessible.

Priority:

High

---

## 14. Rating & Reviews Section

Display:

* Average Rating
* Total Reviews

Users can:

* View Reviews
* Submit Review
* Give Rating

Business owners can:

* Reply to Reviews

---

# DESIGN RULES

The page must prioritize:

1. Trust
2. Visibility
3. Contact Actions
4. Lead Generation

Not content overload.

Every section should help the user answer:

"Can I trust this business and should I contact them?"
