# NAZDIKAM UX/UI REDESIGN RULES (MANDATORY)

## RULE 1 — Authentication Must Be Visible

Current Problem:
Users do not understand whether they are logged in or not.

Requirements:

* Login status must always be visible.
* Guest users must see:

  * ورود / ثبت نام
* Logged-in users must see:

  * Avatar
  * Name
  * Account menu
* Any action requiring authentication must redirect to login and then return the user to the original page.

Examples:

* Save Business
* Save Product
* Save Service
* Follow Business
* Submit Review
* Consultation Request
* Price Inquiry

---

## RULE 2 — User Dashboard And Business Dashboard Must Be Connected

Current Problem:
Business owners cannot easily return to their personal account area.

Requirements:

Account Structure:

User Account
├── Profile
├── Saved Items
├── Following
├── Reviews
├── Requests
└── My Businesses

If the user owns at least one business:

User Account
└── My Businesses
├── Business A
├── Business B
└── Create Business

Business Dashboard must always contain:

← Return To Personal Account

Personal Account must always contain:

→ Go To Business Dashboard

Users must never feel trapped in one dashboard.

---

## RULE 3 — Mobile First Design

Target Audience:

* Business Owners
* Consumers

Primary Device:

Mobile Phone

Requirements:

* Every screen designed for mobile first.
* Desktop is an expansion of mobile.
* No desktop-only workflow.
* No hidden mobile features.

---

## RULE 4 — Visual Consistency Across Devices

Desktop and Mobile must share:

* Same Design Language
* Same Color System
* Same Component System
* Same Navigation Logic

Desktop must not look like a different product.

The user should immediately recognize the same experience on every device.

---

## RULE 5 — Products And Services Are One Content Type

Current Problem:
Products and Services are treated differently.

New Rule:

Product and Service detail pages must use exactly the same layout.

Only labels and business logic differ.

The user should not need to learn two interfaces.

---

# PRODUCT / SERVICE DETAIL PAGE STRUCTURE

1. Image Slider

2. Product / Service Title

3. Category + Location Box

Display only:

* Province
* City

4. Business Box

Contains:

* Business Logo
* Business Name
* Follower Count
* Follow Button (+)

5. Pricing Box

Contains:

* Final Price
* Discount Percentage
* Original Price (strikethrough)

6. Installment Section

Card 1:

* Number of installments
* Installment amount
* Total installment cost

Card 2:

* Eligible groups
  Examples:

  * Retirees
  * Women
  * Men

Card 3:

* Short installment conditions

7. Action Buttons

Two equal buttons:

* Directions
* Phone Call

Both connected to the business profile.

8. Description Section

Full details.

9. Reviews Section

Users can:

* Rate
* Comment
* Read reviews

This structure must be identical for both Products and Services.

---

# PRODUCT / SERVICE CARD RULES

Used on:

* Home
* Categories
* Search
* Business Profile
* Offers
* Any listing

Cards must remain compact.

Display ONLY:

* Image
* Title
* Discount Percentage
* Installment Count
* Final Price
* Original Price

DO NOT display:

* Long descriptions
* Installment details
* Business information
* Full specifications
* Review blocks

The purpose of cards is discovery.

The purpose of detail pages is evaluation.
